Olympiads

My Journey in Olympiads and other STEM Comeptetion

Since childhood, I have participated science competitions, at school, regional, national, and international levels. Astronomy Olympiads, in particular, have been a significant part of my journey. I have represented Bangladesh in International Olympiad on Astronomy and Astrophysics. (IOAA 24)

Honors and Awards in Astronomy & Astrophysics

  • Bangladesh Team, IOAA 2024
  • Gold Medal, Bangladesh Astronomy Olympiad (BAO) 2023 – Ranked 3rd in the whole country
  • Global Nominee – NASA SPACE APPS CHALLENGE 2025
  • IAAC Gold Honor (2023, 2024)
